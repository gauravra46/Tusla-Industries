U Bond 455

Heavy Duty Wood Adhesive

High solid Milky white emulsion, based on Vinyl acetate specially formulated for all types of wood bonding where a fast set and high bond strength is required. It is recommended as general purpose glue for wood and packing items.

Key Features & Propertise

  • VOC Compliant
  • Easy Application and Clean Up
  • Solvent–free
  • Excellent bonding properties
  • Suitable for material under tension
  • Temperature resistant from -40C to + 60C
  • Coverage 150 ml/m²
  • Open Time 5-7 min
