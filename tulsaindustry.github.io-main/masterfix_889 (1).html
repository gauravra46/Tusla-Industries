Masterfix 889

Pedstal Adhesive

Masterfix 889is specifically designed for the installation of metal pedestals to concrete floors. Surfaces to which pedestals are to be bonded must be clean, dry, free of dust, dirt, paint, oils, wax and any other foreign matter. Apply adhesive to pedestal by the method normally used by a professional installer of access flooring. Full coverage of the pedestal is recommended with adhesive exuding from the pedestal base perimeter. Keep container sealed when not in use. Clean up with warm water while adhesive is still wet.

Key Features & Propertise

  • VOC Compliant
  • Easy Application and Clean Up
  • Solvent–free
  • Excellent bonding properties
  • Suitable for material under tension
  • Temperature resistant from -40C to + 60C
  • Coverage 150 ml/m²
  • Open Time 5-7 min
